Site Purpose

This site will offer information about the city of Orlando, Florida. There will be a place to see events, accomplishments, good news, upcoming information, and more. There will also be a "suggestion box" for residents and a place where they can submit photos of their adventures around the city. The site will join community together while providing information about what is currently happening around town.
